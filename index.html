Second-hand Car Sales Market Price

Used car market is a popular market. For potential customers, used cars are at an affordable price. For car owners, it is important to know the depraciation and the maintanence cost per year to find out a good time to sell a car.

Carmax, Kelley Blue Book and Edmunds are companies that accept and sell used cars based in the United States. Such used vehicle business companies have built models for fair valuation of used cars. For example, Edmunds has such an appraisal model based on manufacture year, make, model and more information.

We have collected used vehicle information, such as price, mileage, make, model and manufacture year, for brands Audi,Ford and Volkswagen.

The following data visualization supports general correlations between mileage, manufacture year and price.

Click here if you are interested in a specific car brand. Audi. Volkswagen. Ford.

The way that Mileage Afffects Price

  • At the same level of mileage, the price of Audi make vehicle is higher than that of Ford or Volkswagen make vehicle, showing an advantage of brand value.

  • As the mileage increases, the price has a linearly decreasing trend.
